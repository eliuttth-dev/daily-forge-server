PRINCIPAL FEATURE BRANCH: feature/#8-habit-tracking

MAIN USER STORY: As a user, I want to undo a habit entry in case I mark it by mistake so that my habit streak remains accurate.

SUB-TASKS:

[] - Undo Endpoint for Habit Entry – Allow users to quickly undo a habit entry right after marking it.
[] - Habit History Log – Maintain a log of all habit completions with timestamps, enabling users to revert changes.
[] - Confirm Undo Prompt – Ask users for confirmation before undoing an entry to prevent accidental changes.
[] - Streak Recalculation – Ensure that streaks are dynamically updated when a habit entry is undone.
[] - Analytics & Logs for Admins – Store habit undo actions in the backend for audit purposes and debugging.
